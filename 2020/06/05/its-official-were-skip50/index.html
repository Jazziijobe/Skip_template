It’s official… WE’RE SKIP50!

By Sophie | June 5, 2020

The time has finally come for us to share our exciting news – our new brand, Skip50 has officially launched! There’s exciting goals, stories and values attached to our new company name, brand and structure and we can’t wait to share finally them with you all…

Our journey…

With a BA (Hons) in photography and over 8 years professional experience, creative entrepreneur Sophie Skipper made the exciting leap to start her own business and founded Sophie Skipper Photography in February 2016. With the mission to inspire businesses to celebrate their journey through regular and consistently AWESOME visual content, the business grew beyond expectations. We are thrilled to have had the opportunity to work with so many wonderful brands and teams!

In April 2019, the team began to grow and by mid-January we were a mighty team of 4 with expertise in photography, videography, retouching, marketing and production. It was at this point where Sophie felt she needed to take some time to make positive changes as we look to take the business to the next level.

After much planning and development, Skip50 came to life with the dream of becoming the ‘partner in visual content’ to brands across the UK and beyond! We have every intention to continue this rate of growth going forward! Make sure you keep your eyes peeled for further updates!

Our NEW subscription packages…

We have repositioned some of our services to make sure we are offering you the tools needed to advance your business. With that said, we are thrilled to unveil our brand new subscription packages that save you time and money and provide you with consistently AWESOME visual content for all your marketing needs.

 

 

Our flagship package – AWESOME visual content, all year around…

We have created an exclusive subscription service, tailored to supporting our ‘partners in content’ by offering a hassle-free, flexible solution that is shaped to fit your business activities. We are regularly asked about the 50 in our name…within this package we work with a maximum of 50 partners per year to ensure 100% dedication and energy.

We work with your marketing department (as if we were an extension of it!) and plan your visual content strategy for the coming year. Through engaging photography, videography, motion graphics and design, we will pinpoint the areas YOUR clients want to hear about and give you the tools for exceptional delivery. Don’t have a marketing department? No problem, the Bundle can be adapted to include marketing, strategy & social media should you require it.

The beauty of all of our subscription services is they are tailored to you. After new team headshots in January and have a new product launch in Feb…? The Skip50 Bundle is flexible and ensures you have all the content you need to manage your marketing. Our flagship package is also fully customisable with optional add-ons and upgrade options if you’re after extra hours in a particular area (at a discounted rate!).
