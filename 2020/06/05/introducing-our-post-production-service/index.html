Introducing… OUR POST-PRODUCTION SERVICE!

By Laura | June 5, 2020

Although we hate to dwell on it, we are currently working within unprecedented times and so many companies, influencers and professionals are creating their own content right now. As part of our rebrand process (we are now Skip50 – woooo!), we have repositioned some of our services to make sure we are offering our ‘partners in visual content’ the tools needed to keep their marketing hot during these uncertain times, even when budgets are tight.

With that said, we are pleased to share details our brand-new post-production & social content subscriptions! DRUM ROLL PLEASE…

Post-production & Photography

Is regular content important for your online business? Have you recently set up a product business that needs regular imagery? We understand that even when budgets are tight, high-end content is still super important, so we’ve created a service that can help you get the most out of your self-shot images.

Submit your photos to us and we’ll edit them for you! Receive unlimited photo edits each month for only £99. Our post-production services include; colour corrections, cropping & re-sizing, exposure corrections and blemish/object removal. Image masking (background removal) is also available for an additional £85 per month on up to 100 images.

Post-production & Videography

Showcase your offering, inject personality and build trust, but boy, can it be time consuming! Submit your videos to us and we’ll save you hours of time by editing them for you! We’ll add subtitles, branding and text graphics alongside optimising your videos for your preferred social media platforms.

We’re offering UNLIMITED video edits for only £125 per month! Just send us your raw video and we’ll do the rest.

Post-production & Social Content

Wanting to make the most of your video, blog or article? Submit a blog, video, article or script and we’ll take your content, edit it, brand it up and transform it into 5 other pieces of online content just for you. Enjoy unlimited submissions for just £275 per month. A stress-free process, tonnes of time saved and engaging content with a professional feel for all your social platforms!
