CASE STUDY

Food Innovation Solutions (FIS) is the brainchild of Managing Director – Mike Faers, having identified a burning need for innovation amongst many businesses operating in the food and beverage sector. They provide food and beverage solutions that excite, inspire and resonate with real customers. They stimulate imaginations and create unique recipes, dishes and products that add end-to-end value for start-ups right through to global household brands.

Whether you’re launching a new restaurant, relaunching a menu, need help creating a new recipe or product or looking to optimise processes, FIS can help businesses achieve ground-breaking results. They’re incredibly hands on and only create things that they would like to look at, touch and taste themselves.

HOW DID WE CONNECT?

Whether you’re launching a new menu, starting a new restaurant or simply need help creating new recipes, Food Innovation Solutions are a London based creative team providing food and beverage innovation solutions to some of the best know food/drink brands around!

We met with FIS through successful projects completed for their sister company Good Sense Research. The key ingredients for FIS when it came to their visual content needs were excitement, innovation and vibrancy. The team were after some exciting commercial photography and video to showcase their central London kitchen, their talented team and their incredible dishes & recipes.

WHAT DID WE DO?

Over the course of 3 shoots, we created a portfolio of content for FIS to use across their online platforms and within their marketing. This archive of goodness included:

– Some seriously punchy food photography full of colour, life and movement!

– Team headshots with a difference

– Action shots of the FIS chef’s doing what they do best!

– An engaging brand video created to show the world what FIS are about, how they are innovating the food sector and highlight their creativity and talent.

The visual content created for FIS has been used across their varying online platforms and within their print marketing solutions.
