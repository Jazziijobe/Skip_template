Our story so far...

With a BA (Hons) in photography and over 5 years professional experience, creative entrepreneur Sophie Skipper made the exciting leap to start her own business and founded Skip50 in February 2016.

With the mission to inspire businesses to celebrate their journey through regular and consistently AWESOME visual content, our mighty team of 4 offers client’s expertise in photography, videography, retouching, marketing and production. We will work with you on your creative strategy and handle the logistics, development and post-production in-house.

It’s been an exciting journey. Like with any growing business, there’s ups and downs, learning curves and obstacles to overcome, but the Skip50 team have seen year on year growth and we are thrilled to have had the opportunity to work with so many wonderful brands and teams. A big ‘cheers’ to our partners in content!

Sophie

Company Director & Photographer

Photography has been a part of Sophie’s life since she was a child. After gaining her BA (Hons) in Documentary Photography at the University of South Wales, Sophie worked across a number of photography roles in London and Essex, including a rather graphic stint as a Medical Photographer at Basildon Hospital! Despite gathering some great experience, these roles weren’t quite ticking all of the boxes. Sophie knew she wanted to aim high and create something a little bit different, so started her business in February 2016 offering an alternative approach to commercial photography for businesses!
